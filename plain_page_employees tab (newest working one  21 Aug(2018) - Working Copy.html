Misconduct Catagories

Misconduct catagories is the first broad groups into which the different types of misconducts are divided.

The reason for this differentiation is the legal principle of progressive dicipline. In terms of this principle misconduct by employees must be dealt with in a progressive manner by issuing verbal, written and final written warnings to offending employees depending on the sevarity of the offence comitted. Warnings for lesser offences therefore start with verbal warings for the first offence and progress to written and final written warnings for subsequent offences.

What is important in this regard is that only warnings warnings that are issued for same of similar types of offences may build on each other. Seesa ProFile assists you with this process by keeping track of the types of warnings issued to employees.

Please select a Misconduct Catagorie below to learn more about the different types of Misconducts.
